The Financial Market

The foreign exchange market is the market in which participants are able to buy, sell, exchange and speculate on currencies. Knowing it's core functions will help in making trading decisions.

Participants Of The Forex Market

There are more parties that trade currencies spanning many completely different reasons than those in the stock market. Therefore, it is very important to identify and understand the functions and motivations of these main players in the forex market.

The Economy- Fundamentals

The economy is the production and consumption activities that determine how scarce resources are allocated in an area. Being knowledgeable about the overall well being of a country will allow you to tell if a currency value will decrease or increase. This turns into more profitable trades

Central Banks & Monetary Policy

A central bank or monetary authority is the institution that manages the currency, money supply, and interest rates of a state or formal monetary union. Knowing how to trace this can help in know the long term market directions.

Learn more

Investment Instruments

Financial instruments are assets that can be traded. They can also be seen as packages of capital that may be traded. Most types of financial instruments provide an efficient flow and transfer of capital all throughout the world's investors.

Learn more

Role of Brokers

A broker is an individual or firm that charges a fee or commission for executing buy and sell orders submitted by an investor. Choosing the right broker is also a very important start as a trader/investor. We will give you guidelines of what to look for.
